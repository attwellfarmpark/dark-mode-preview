Design Notes

Key Principles

  • No pure black — backgrounds use dark green (primary-950) to maintain brand identity
  • No pure white text — off-white with warm tint reduces eye strain
  • Lighter accents — primary shifts from 400→300, secondary from 300→200 for contrast on dark
  • Elevation via lightness — higher surfaces are lighter, not shadowed
  • Brand-tinted darks — all surfaces carry a subtle green hue (162)
